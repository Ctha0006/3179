Categories

Lives lost in Victorian workplace have been recorded by WorkSafe Victoria whcih previously excluded some work-related deaths that were not attributable to duties or obligations under the Occupational Health and Safety Act 2004, such as those caused by transport accidents or occupational diseases. Now workers who die on the road, suicides attributable to work, industrial diseases such as silicosis, and workplace deaths resulting from a criminal act, are included. We implemented this change for reporting for the 2020-21 financial year, commencing 1 July 2020.
